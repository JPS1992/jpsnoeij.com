Frontier AI Diplomacy

I work on frontier AI diplomacy at the Simon Institute for Longterm Governance in Geneva, where I focus on fostering international cooperation to navigate transformative AI responsibly.

My work sits at the intersection of technology governance and international relations — helping bridge the gap between technical realities and policy responses during this pivotal moment for humanity.

Beyond work, I'm drawn to what contemplative traditions reveal about the mind, how complex systems evolve, and the flourishing of sentient beings through the lenses of economics, history, and societal resilience.
